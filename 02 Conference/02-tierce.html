Y.E.T.I. Fan Con 1999

Attention, fans of The Man From Y.E.T.I. — registration is now open for the 3rd Annual Y.E.T.I. Fan Con! Join us July 16th – 18th at the Quality Inn O'Hare Airport in Chicago for three days dedicated to one of the greatest television shows ever created! Read on to learn more about this venue, the incredible events we have scheduled, and register to make sure you get a ticket when they become available.

Location

Set 2.2 miles from O'Hare International Airport, this unassuming hotel is 3 miles from the Donald Stephens Convention Center and 7 miles from Allstate Arena.
